FAQ

Frequently asked.

  • How long does a camera repair take?

    Most repairs are completed in 3 to 5 business days. Complex jobs requiring parts may take 7 to 10 business days. We share a diagnostic and quote within one business day.

  • Do you repair film cameras?

    Yes. We service vintage and modern film cameras including Pentax, Nikon F-series, Canon AE-1, Leica M, Minolta, Yashica, and more. Internal cleaning, lubrication, light seal replacement, and CLA service available.

  • What brands do you service?

    Canon, Nikon, Sony, Fujifilm, Leica, Panasonic, Olympus, Sigma, Tamron, DJI, GoPro, Profoto, and most major photography and lighting brands.

  • How much does sensor cleaning cost?

    Sensor cleaning starts at $49 for most APS-C and full-frame cameras. Medium format and specialty sensors may vary. We include a 30-day free re-clean guarantee.

  • Do you offer a warranty?

    Yes. Every repair carries a six-month warranty covering the specific work performed. Sensor cleaning includes a 30-day free re-clean.

  • Can I ship my camera in?

    Absolutely. Use our Send Your Equipment process — print a prepaid label, drop the package, and track every step. We also offer on-site mobile service across Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, and Monroe counties.
