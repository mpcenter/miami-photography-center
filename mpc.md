What does the On-Site service include?

Our On-Site service is designed to bring professional technical maintenance directly to your location — whether it’s your home, studio, or production set.

Includes:

Professional Sensor Cleaning: thorough cleaning of the sensor, body, one lens, and one additional accessory. Lubrication, full exterior maintenance, and a firmware update (if applicable) are also included. Completed in approximately 30 minutes.

Minor repairs and equipment pickup: we can perform basic services on-site or pick up your gear for workshop repair and return it ready to use.

Free diagnostics and estimates at events: during conferences, meetups, or photography gatherings, we offer complimentary evaluations and provide estimates on the spot.

Scheduled visits for groups: available across South Florida and surrounding areas (minimum of 20 equipment items required).

Custom maintenance plans: designed for individual creators, businesses, educational institutions, and production companies.

Note: A travel fee may apply depending on your location.

What types of equipment do you repair?

At Miami Photography Center, we offer full technical service for a wide range of photographic and audiovisual equipment, both digital and analog.

Includes:

DSLR, mirrorless, cinema, and medium format cameras (digital and film)

Lenses (zoom and prime, manual or autofocus)

Flashes, lights, triggers, monitors, and recording units

Accessories such as tripods, grips, eyecups, straps, adapters, and more

We work with leading brands such as Canon, Nikon, Sony, Fujifilm, Panasonic, Blackmagic, and many others.

All our services come with a 6-month warranty.

Is it safe to use professionally repaired cameras for commercial work?

Yes. A professionally repaired camera, serviced by experienced technicians, is fully capable of delivering top-level performance — even in demanding environments.

Our maintenance and repair processes ensure:

Accurate focus, exposure, and mechanical function

Stable performance during long recordings

Reliable operation under professional use conditions

Most repairs are completed in about one week, depending on the initial evaluation and parts availability.

Includes:

Full technical diagnosis and inspection

Ordering and replacement of parts, if needed

Cleaning, calibration, and complete testing

We keep our clients informed throughout the entire repair process.

Do you use original replacement parts?

Yes. All of our repairs are carried out using original manufacturer components, or certified equivalents when OEM parts are not available.

This ensures:

Full compatibility with your equipment

Long-term durability and stability

Preservation of your professional performance and value.

All our repairs include a 6-month warranty covering both labor and parts.
