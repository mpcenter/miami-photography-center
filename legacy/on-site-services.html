FAQ

On-site questions.

  • What does on-site service include?

    Sensor cleaning, lens calibration, focus testing, minor mechanical adjustments, firmware updates, and full diagnostic. Complex repairs requiring parts come back to our workshop.

  • How fast can you arrive?

    Same-day for emergencies in Miami-Dade and Broward when slots are available. Standard appointments within 24 to 72 hours across South Florida. Production sets and weddings get priority dispatch.

  • Where do you cover?

    Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, and Monroe counties — from West Palm Beach to Key West. Travel fees vary by zone.

  • Can you service on a film set?

    Yes. We work around your call sheet. Discreet setup, anti-static workflow, and we move with your production. Sensor cleaning typically done between takes.

  • What does it cost?

    Service fee starts at $79 plus travel by zone. Miami-Dade and Broward zones from $25, Palm Beach $50, Florida Keys custom. Multiple cameras and bulk discounts available.

  • Do you bring your own tools?

    Yes. Calibrated cleaning station, anti-static gear, sensor swabs in every size, air blowers, sensor loupes, lens calibration charts, ColorChecker, and diagnostic toolkit. Everything you’d find at our workshop, portable.
